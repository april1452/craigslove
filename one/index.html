Blogpost #1

We used sklearn library to assign TF/IDF weights to all our craigslist personal ads, then perform k-means clustering. So far we played around with clustering only the Providence posts, because the data from all the cities combined was too big (for now). Here are the results from 2-means clustering (i.e. the top terms used for each of 2 clusters) which are pretty interesting:
top terms for cluster 0:
host clean cock stats suck discreet free nice
top terms for cluster 1:
love fun time interested meet nice email real

Here are the results from another iteration, of 5-means clustering. It seems like there is a lot of overlap.
